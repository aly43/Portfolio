Field or Career

As of 10/31/21 I am in my final semester at the University of Pittsburgh. I am majoring in CS, however my true goal is to make enough money and then become a commercial pilot. It's not that I dislike SWE, I just feel like I am more passionate about being an air bus-driver. I used to play Ultimate Frisbee, and I used to be pretty good. I made the A team here at Pitt which is ranked top 10 nationally, however because of the pandemic, I lost all passion I had for the game so I quit. Currently, I am working part time at a financial institute as a software engineer.
